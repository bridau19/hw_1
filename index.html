Laura Riding


On Falsity

yes — there are also false differences of understanding.

there are inventors of difference

bent greedily on having their own to say…

who might press forward

to have a part in the making of our truth

stealing the name of it for their inventions




On Truth

there remain still to be told the fundamentals of our being

we are the natural tellers of them

each a natural teller of a story of which we and Everything, together, are the Subject

the story of ourselves and everything that touches on us, everything we touch on…

the task of truth

is divided among us

to the number of us...
